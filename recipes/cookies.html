Chewy Cookie Recipe

This recipe and image both originated from https://tasty.co/recipe/the-best-chewy-chocolate-chip-cookies

The very best cookies

These are by far the best cookies I have ever made and this recipe is very easy to follow.

If, like me, you love chewy, guewy, chocolate chip cookies, you will love these cookes for sure.

Ingredients

For 12 Cookies

  • 100g granulated sugar
  • 165g brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 115g melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 155g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 220g chocholate chunks

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until a paste forms with no lumps.
  2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short while before falling back into the mixture.
  3. Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill for at least 30 minutes.
  5. preheat oven to 180 C. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Scoop the dough onto the parchment paper, leaving at least 4 inches of space between cookies and 2 inches of space from the edge of the pan. They will spread!
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges have started to barely brown.
  8. Cool completely before serving
